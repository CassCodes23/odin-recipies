Ham and Cheese Sliders

Description

These ham and cheese sliders are made with Swiss cheese, honey Dijon Mustard, and poppy seed dressing, and baked on Hawaiian rolls. With just 6 ingredients, I can make these hot ham sliders in the oven with ease.

Ingredients

  • 12 Hawaiian sweet rolls (one package)
  • 9 slices Ham
  • 9 slices Swiss Cheese
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup poppyseed dressing

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. In a bowl, combine the Dijon and honey and stir until smooth.
    • 1/4 cup Honey
    • 1/4 cup Dijon Mustard
  3. Cut the rolls in half length-wise. Place the bottoms of the 12 rolls in a lightly sprayed baking dish (do not separate them).
    • 12 Hawaiian Sweet Rolls
  4. Brush the rolls with honey Dijon.
  5. Top the bottom layer of rolls with slices of ham and then slices of cheese.
    • 9 slices of Ham
    • 9 slices Swiss Cheese
  6. Brush the inside of the top half of rolls with more honey Dijon, then place on top.
  7. Liberally brush the outside of each top with poppy seed dressing.
    • 1/4 cup poppyseed dressing
  8. Cover the dish with foil and cook for 15-18 minutes, or until cheese is melty.
  9. Broil for 4 minutes or until rolls are slightly browned/crisped.
  10. Serve immediately while still hot!
